## 1. Getting started

### Sign up or log in

When you first open Sprout you'll land on the welcome screen.

- **New here?** Tap **Sign up**. You'll need a name, an email, and a password (at least 8 characters).
- **Already have an account?** Tap **Log in**.

If something looks off, Sprout will tell you in plain language — `"that email doesn't look right"`, `"that password's a bit short"`, `"there's already a sprout for that email"`. No red error walls.

### Onboarding (first launch only)

After signup you'll go through four quick steps:

1. **Welcome** — meet the sprout mascot.
2. **Personality quiz** — pick what fits best:
   - **Saver** — every euro has a job.
   - **Treater** — life's for living, within reason.
   - **Goal chaser** — chasing something specific.
   - **Impulse** — figuring it out as you go.
3. **Budget** — pick your currency (EUR or USD) and your monthly budget.
4. **First goal** — name it, pick an amount, pick a deadline (optional).

That's it. You're in.

## 2. The five tabs

The bottom bar has five tabs, left to right:

| Tab | What lives here |
|---|---|
| 🏠 **Home** | Today's balance, daily nudge, today's transactions, the **+** button to log a spend. |
| 📊 **Stats** | Charts of spending by category, calendar heatmap, mood patterns. |
| 🎯 **Goals** | Your savings goals — personal and shared. |
| 🌿 **Together** | Your partner dashboard. Shown only when you're partnered. |
| 👤 **You** | Profile, budget, theme, language, cooldown, round-up, partner panel, logout. |

Tap a tab to switch. Tabs fade rather than slide — gentler.

## 3. Logging a transaction

The **+** button on Home opens the Add Transaction sheet.

1. **Amount.** Type the cost.
2. **Category.** Tap one of your categories (Groceries, Coffee, Transit, etc.). You can add more later in Categories.
3. **Note** *(optional)* — what was it?
4. **Mood** *(optional)* — how do you feel about this spend?
5. **Save.**

The transaction shows up immediately on Home and counts against your budget.

### Edit or delete a transaction

Tap any transaction on Home → edit fields, or swipe / tap **Delete**.

### Planned (recurring) transactions

When adding a transaction, mark it **planned** if it repeats every month (rent, subscriptions, bus pass). Sprout will make sure a copy exists each month — you don't need to log it again. Editing a planned transaction edits the template.

## 4. Your monthly budget

Your **monthly budget** is your spending balance for the month. Spending pulls from it. Money you set aside into goals **also** pulls from it — they're both moves out of the same pot.

- See it on **Home** as the big balance card.
- The card shows **Effective budget** (your recurring budget **+** any one-off top-up for this month) minus this month's spending minus this month's savings.
- The balance can go **negative**. Sprout won't shame you — just a heads-up.

### Change your recurring monthly budget

**You → Monthly budget**. Set a new amount (e.g. after a raise). It applies from this month onward. Range is 0 to 50,000.

### One-off top-up (bonuses, gifts, side jobs)

Got a one-time €400 from a side gig in June? Don't change your recurring budget — top up just this month.

**You → Top up this month** → enter the amount. It lifts only this month's effective budget. Future months stay as they were.

### Over budget?

When any single **category** goes over its monthly cap, Sprout shows an over-budget chip on Home. The overall budget going negative is a separate signal on the balance card.

## 5. Categories

A category bundles related spending (Groceries, Coffee, Transit, Going out, …).

Each category has:

- **Name + icon + color** — make it yours.
- **Monthly cap** — when you blow through it, Sprout shows a gentle nudge.
- **Share with partner** *(only relevant when partnered)* — see §13.

### Add a category

From the Add Transaction sheet, tap **+ New category**. Or from a category page → **Add category**.

### Edit / delete

Tap a category → edit cap, name, icon, color, or delete.

### Tap into a category

Tapping a category opens its detail page: all transactions in that category, this month's total against its cap, and a sparkline of recent activity.

## 6. Goals & saving

A **goal** is something you're saving toward — a holiday, an emergency fund, a new bike.

Each goal has:

- **Name, icon, color**
- **Target amount** — how much you need.
- **Current amount** — how much you've saved so far.
- **Deadline** *(optional)* — Sprout shows a countdown.
- **Boosts** — every time you set money aside.

### Create a goal

**Goals tab → +** → name, target, optional deadline, icon, color, save.

### Set aside money toward a goal

From the goal's detail page → **Set aside €X** → enter an amount. The goal's `currentAmount` goes up and your monthly budget balance goes down (because you spent it on Future-You).

### Milestones & confetti

Goals celebrate at **25%, 50%, 75% and 100%** — confetti + the sprout mascot fist-pumping. The first goal you complete unlocks an achievement badge (§11).

### Edit / delete

From the goal detail page → ⋯ menu.

> Shared goals work differently — see §13.4.

## 7. Round-up savings

Sprout can quietly grow your primary goal every time you spend.

**How it works.** Spend €3.40 on coffee → round-up = €0.60 → that €0.60 boosts your primary goal automatically.

### Turn it on

**You → Round-up savings → on**.

The "primary goal" is the first personal goal in your list. If you don't have one, round-ups don't fire.

### On shared transactions

If you're partnered and you log a shared transaction:

- Round-up only fires when **you** are the payer.
- It rounds **your share**, not the full amount.
- Your partner's round-up settings only ever affect their own goals.

## 8. Cooldown — the 30-second breath

The cooldown is a gentle pause before logging a bigger-than-usual spend. It's optional.

### Turn it on

**You → Cooldown timer → on**.

Pick a **threshold** — e.g. 30. Anything you log at or above that amount triggers a 30-second breath screen before saving.

During the breath you can:

- **Wait it out and log it** — totally fine, it was a thoughtful spend.
- **Cancel** — you talked yourself out of it.

### Cooldown wins

Every cancel:

- Adds the avoided amount to your **lifetime cooldown saved** (visible on the You tab).
- Bumps your **cooldown wins** counter.
- Counts toward the `cooldowns10` achievement (§11).

Planned transactions skip the cooldown — you already decided.

## 9. Streaks & no-spend days

Sprout rewards the **habit** of checking in, not the size of any spend.

Your **streak** counts consecutive days that you either:

- logged at least one transaction, **or**
- marked the day as a **no-spend day**.

### See your streak

The 🔥 chip on Home shows your current streak. Tap it to see your longest streak too.

### Mark today no-spend

If you didn't spend anything today, tap **Mark no-spend** from Home (or from the streak chip). Today instantly counts toward your streak.

Already logged a transaction today? You don't need to do anything — the day already counts.

### Streak rules

- Streaks reset if a day passes without either a transaction or a no-spend flag.
- Hit 7 days → you unlock the `streak7` achievement.
- Streaks are **personal**. A shared transaction with your partner does **not** count toward their streak.

## 10. Daily nudges & insights

### Daily nudge (Home banner)

Sprout reads your day so far and shows one of three nudges:

- **No-spend** — `"a calm day. nice."`
- **Ahead of pace** — `"you're €X ahead of today's pace"` (your daily target is `monthlyBudget / daysInMonth`).
- **Treat day** — `"already above today's pace — make it count."`

Never shaming. Just a friendly check-in.

### Insights (Stats tab)

Sprout surfaces patterns over time:

- **Latte factor** — small recurring spends that add up.
- **Mood pattern** — what you spend on when you're feeling a certain way.
- **Weekly recap** — your week, summarized.
- **Achievement unlock** — a celebration card when you earn a badge.

Insights refresh in the background; you don't need to do anything.

---

## 11. Achievements

Sprout has four badges, all on the You tab. They unlock automatically:

| Badge | Unlocks when |
|---|---|
| 💰 **First €100 saved** | Your goal balance crosses €100. |
| 🔥 **7-day streak** | Your current or longest streak hits 7. |
| 🌱 **10 cooldown wins** | You've cancelled 10 cooldowns. |
| 🎉 **First goal hit** | You complete any goal. |

Badges are derived from your activity — there's nothing to claim.

## 12. Stats

The Stats tab is your read-only overview.

- **Donut chart** — spending by category this month.
- **Bar chart** — spending day-by-day or week-by-week.
- **Calendar heatmap** — darker = more spent. White squares are no-spend days.
- **Category sparklines** — quick trends per category.
- **Mood breakdown** — if you've been tagging moods.
- **Insight cards** — see §10.

Tap any chart segment for a deeper view.

## 13. Partner Plan

The Partner Plan lets you link 1:1 with another Sprout user — a partner, a flatmate, a sibling — to plan shared money. **Your personal finances stay private by default.**

### 13.1 What your partner can see

Only:

1. **Shared goals** you jointly own.
2. **Shared transactions** you marked shared at the moment you logged them.
3. **Monthly totals** of categories you've explicitly set to share — the **sum only**, never individual transactions.

What your partner **cannot** see: your overall monthly spend, your total savings, your monthly budget, your personal goals, your personal transactions, or any category you haven't opted to share. Same the other way.

### 13.2 Invite a partner

**You → Link Partner → invite by email**.

- If they have a Sprout account, an invite is sent.
- If they don't, Sprout will tell you: `"no sprout account for that email — invite them to sign up first"`.

While the invite is pending you'll see `"waiting on <email> to accept"` with a **Cancel invite** link.

Invites expire after **7 days** if not accepted.

### 13.3 Accept (or decline) an invite

When someone invites you, an accept/decline card appears on the You tab the next time you open Sprout:

> *"<name> wants to grow money with you. join?"*

Tap **yes, let's** or **not now**. You can also decline an invite that came in by mistake.

Once you accept, both of you see the **Together** tab and the linked state.

### 13.4 Shared goals

A **shared goal** is co-owned with your partner — a holiday fund, a deposit, the kids' summer camp.

**Create one:** Goals tab → **+ Shared goal** (only visible when partnered). You pick:

- The goal details (name, target, deadline, icon, color), same as a personal goal.
- The **funding mode**:
  - **Split contributions** — each of you boosts from your own money. The goal tracks who contributed what. Two stacked bars show each partner's running contribution.
  - **Shared pot** — you deposit into one communal balance. Either of you can deposit.

Tap a shared goal to open its detail page:

- **Split contributions** — see each partner's contribution and the boost history with avatars.
- **Shared pot** — see the pot balance + **Deposit** button. Deposits go into the pot.

Both partners see milestone confetti at 25/50/75/100%.

### 13.5 Shared transactions

When you log a transaction and you're partnered, the sheet shows a **Share with partner** toggle.

Toggling it on reveals:

- **Split slider** — default 50/50, snapping to 5% increments.
- **Paid by** — segmented control: **me** or **partner**.

The math:

- A €60 grocery shop, 50/50, paid by you → €30 counts against your budget; €30 against your partner's; the full €60 shows in **shared transactions this month** on the Together tab.
- A 70/30 split, paid by your partner → your partner's budget bears their share; yours bears yours.

Sprout does **not** track "you owe me €X" balances. Reconciling between partners is left to you.

### 13.6 Share a category total

In the category editor, toggle **Share total with partner**. Your partner will see that category's **monthly total** on their Together tab — never the underlying transactions.

Default for all categories (including ones you create after linking) is **off**. Sharing is always opt-in, per category.

### 13.7 The Together tab

Only visible when partnered. Shows shared-only data:

- **Header** — `"you & <partnerName>"`.
- **Shared goals** summary (top 3 by recency, link to full list on the Goals tab).
- **Shared transactions this month** — total + the list of shared transactions.
- **Shared categories** — each opted-in category from either partner with this month's total, in your currency.
- **Weekly recap** — `"this week you both saved <€X> toward <goalName>"`.

If your partner uses a different currency, totals are converted using a daily rate and tagged with **"rates as of <date>"**.

### 13.8 Unlink

**You → Link Partner → Unlink**. You'll be asked to resolve each shared goal:

- **Assign to me** — it becomes a personal goal of yours.
- **Assign to partner** — it leaves your view (your partner keeps it).
- **Delete** — gone from both views.

After unlinking:

- Every previously-shared category reverts to private.
- Previously-shared transactions keep their split (history is immutable).
- You can link with someone else later.

### 13.9 Partner voice (what you'll see)

- Invite sent: `"sprout's on its way to <email> 🌱"`
- Invite accepted: `"you two are now growing together. nice."`
- Pot deposit: `"<€X> dropped into the pot. it's growing."`
- Shared milestone: `"you both did this. <goalName> is <pct>% there ✨"`
- Goal complete: `"you grew <goalName> all the way. proud of you both 🌳"`
- Unlink confirm: `"sure? you'll keep your own stuff, just stop sharing totals."`

## 14. Settings & profile (the You tab)

The **You** tab is mission control.

| Section | What you can do |
|---|---|
| **Profile** | Edit your name, email. |
| **Monthly budget** | Change your recurring budget; top up this month. |
| **Currency** | Switch between EUR and USD. |
| **Language** | English or Bulgarian. Defaults to your device's language. |
| **Theme** | Light is the default. (Dark mode is coming.) |
| **Cooldown timer** | On/off + threshold (§8). Shows lifetime cooldown savings + win count. |
| **Round-up savings** | On/off (§7). |
| **Achievements** | Your 4 badges (§11). |
| **Link Partner** | Invite, accept/decline, unlink (§13). |
| **Logout** | Signs you out. Your data stays safe on the server. |

## 15. Privacy at a glance

- Your transactions, personal goals, monthly budget, and overall totals are **never** visible to your partner.
- A partner only sees: shared goals, shared transactions, and totals of categories you've explicitly opted to share.
- Your auth session is the only thing stored on this device. Everything else lives on the backend.
- Logging out clears the session token from this device.

## 16. Troubleshooting

**The app won't load past the splash screen.**
The backend can't be reached. Check the device's network, then try restarting the app.

**"that email doesn't look right" when I sign up.**
The email format is invalid. Re-type it carefully.

**"there's already a sprout for that email."**
You already have an account — tap **Log in** instead.

**"that password's a bit short."**
Passwords need at least 8 characters.

**Invite says "no sprout account for that email."**
Your partner hasn't signed up yet. Ask them to make an account, then invite again.

**"this invite expired."**
Invites are valid 7 days. Ask them to send a fresh one.

**"that goal isn't a shared pot."**
You tried to deposit into a goal that's funded by **split contributions**. Open the goal and use **Set aside** / **Boost** instead.

**I logged a transaction and it didn't fire round-up.**
Round-up requires:

- Round-up enabled (You tab).
- A primary (personal) goal that isn't already complete.
- For shared transactions, that **you** are the payer.

**My streak reset and I didn't expect it.**
A day passed where you neither logged a transaction nor marked it no-spend. Streaks build from today onward — no penalty.

**Charts look empty.**
The Stats tab needs a few transactions to show patterns. Keep logging — they'll fill in within a few days.
